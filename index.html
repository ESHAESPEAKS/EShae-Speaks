Security Division

Security presence is part of the brand experience. Security members represent the E'Shae Speaks brand while on duty. Professionalism, respect, and positivity are mandatory.

Client Security Outsourcing

  • Services: presence, crowd flow control, guest management, designated positioning, and support for a safe professional environment.
  • Payment: payment is due at the start of the shift unless another agreement is confirmed in advance.
  • Representation: security represents E'Shae Speaks while on duty.

Booking Terms

  • 50% non-refundable deposit secures approved dates.
  • Remaining balance is due prior to event start unless otherwise confirmed in writing.
  • Custom pricing applies to specialty events, adult venues, private themes, and events without a tip jar structure.
  • Please allow 24-48 hours for writing or media deliverables when included.

Policies & Permissions

These policies protect the client, the team, the media process, and the professional standard of E'Shae Productions. Clients should review these terms before submitting a booking request.

Booking and Deposits

Approved bookings require a 50% non-refundable deposit to secure the date. The remaining balance is due before the event begins unless another arrangement is confirmed in writing.

Cancellation and Rescheduling

Deposits are non-refundable. Cancellations made 72 hours or more before the event may be rescheduled once within 30 days, subject to availability. Cancellations within 72 hours forfeit the deposit. Emergencies may be reviewed at discretion.

Public Articles and Editorial Rights

Articles, event recaps, interviews, reviews, spotlights, video descriptions, and features created by E'Shae Productions may be published publicly on the official website, social media platforms, YouTube descriptions, or promotional materials when connected to a booked or approved media service. E'Shae Productions retains editorial discretion over final written presentation.

Name and Identity Permission

Clients may choose whether their real name, Second Life name, stage name, business name, or brand name is mentioned publicly. If a client declines name usage, E'Shae Productions will avoid public name mentions and may use general wording instead.

Media Release

Photos, videos, flyers, screenshots, clips, interviews, or event visuals may be used for promotion, recap content, website placement, social media posts, or Grid Alert coverage when approved through booking, participation, or submitted materials.

Professional Conduct

Respectful communication is required. E'Shae Productions reserves the right to refuse, cancel, pause, or discontinue service due to disrespect, harassment, unsafe conduct, misrepresentation, non-payment, or behavior that negatively impacts the brand or team.
